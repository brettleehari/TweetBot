🎯 Expert Trading Scenarios

Professional-level decision trees and risk management strategies

📈 Bull Market Strategies

Strategy: Trend-following with volume confirmation

Risk Level: Standard (2% max per trade)

Entry Criteria: RSI < 70, high volume, momentum confirmation

Key Principles:

  • Volume confirmation is crucial - price moves without volume are often false signals
  • Position size: 2% risk maximum for confirmed trends
  • RSI levels determine entry timing (<70 optimal, 70-80 partial, >80 wait)
  • Stop loss: -2% for confirmed trends, take profit: +4%

📉 Bear Market Strategies

Strategy: Capital preservation and selective short opportunities

Risk Level: Reduced (0.5-1% max per trade)

Focus: Risk control and drawdown management

Key Principles:

  • Capital preservation is more important than profits during downtrends
  • Extreme volatility (>5%) requires minimal position sizes (0.5%)
  • Tight risk control with quick profit taking on any short positions
  • Cash position preferred during high volatility periods

🌊 Range-Bound Strategies

Strategy: Mean reversion - buy support, sell resistance

Risk Level: Moderate (1-1.5% max per trade)

Range Width: Requires >2% range for profitability

Key Principles:

  • Wide ranges (>5%) offer better risk/reward ratios
  • Buy near support levels, sell near resistance levels
  • Quick scalping strategies for tight ranges (<2%)
  • Range trading requires patience and discipline

🛡️ Risk Management

Focus: Drawdown control and position sizing rules

Max Drawdown: 10% portfolio emergency stop

Daily Loss Limit: 5% maximum per day

Professional Risk Standards:

  • Maximum 2% risk per trade (never exceed)
  • Total portfolio exposure: Maximum 20% in BTC
  • Drawdown limits: <5% normal, 5-10% reduce risk, >10% emergency stop
  • Stop losses always required - no exceptions

📊 Position Sizing

Formula: Position Size = Risk Amount ÷ (Entry Price - Stop Loss)

Account Risk: 2% of total portfolio per trade

Leverage: Maximum 3x (experts prefer 1-3x)

Mathematical Approach:

  • Risk Amount = Account Balance × 2% (never exceed)
  • Price Risk = Entry Price - Stop Loss Price
  • Position Size = Risk Amount ÷ Price Risk
  • Account for leverage restrictions and margin requirements

Example: BTC @ $60,000, Stop @ $58,800

Account: $100,000 | Risk: $2,000 (2%) | Price Risk: $1,200

Position Size: $2,000 ÷ $1,200 = 1.67 BTC
